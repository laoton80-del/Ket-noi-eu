# VIONA Fashion-Tech Asset Direction (AE.3)

**Purpose:** Guide licensed photography and hero artwork for the Human Constellation / Fashion-Tech Home shell.  
**Scope:** Visual placeholders only until assets are reviewed and checked in locally.

## Principles

- No unlicensed remote stock URLs in production UI.
- Production imagery must live under `assets/` (or an approved brand CDN path), with license recorded by ops.
- Hero and card panels may use abstract gradients and typography until real assets ship.

## Recommended photography style

- Dark editorial, high dynamic range, shallow depth of field.
- Warm skin tones against cool shadows; champagne/gold rim light acceptable.
- Vietnamese-global contexts: dignity-first; avoid cliché “tourist postcard only.”

## Hero panorama (slot)

- Wide landscape or cropped editorial portrait collage.
- Safe zone for headline on left; avoid busy detail behind primary copy.
- Slot label in-app: `home.fashionTech.visualInset`.

## Local

- Neighborhood texture: storefronts, community gathering, services — authentic, not staged ads.

## Travel

- Motion and horizon: transit corridors, respectful landmarks, journey silhouettes — no fake booking imagery.

## Academy

- Learning surfaces: family study, calm screens, notebooks — avoid stock “generic laptop smile.”

## Care Heart Fund

- Human warmth without exploiting subjects; community hands/volunteering tone — amounts and outcomes remain honest/pilot-labeled in UI.

## Licensing

- Each file: source, license type, expiry if applicable, stored alongside asset or in brand ops registry.

## Production checklist

- [ ] Local asset paths only (no hardcoded third-party hotlinks).
- [ ] Legal review for identifiable people/locations where required.
- [ ] Contrast check against dark shell and champagne accents.
